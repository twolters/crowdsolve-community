# Lesson 2 \- The roles every startup needs

## **Goal**

Understand the **core operational capabilities** required to get a startup off the ground.

Even the earliest startups require a few fundamental functions.

---

## **The Four Startup Capability Areas**

1️⃣ **Product / R\&D**

* Building the solution  
* Prototyping  
* Technical development  
* Product design

2️⃣ **Market & Sales**

* Understanding customers  
* Creating messaging  
* Finding early adopters  
* Generating revenue

3️⃣ **Financial Understanding**

* Understanding costs  
* Revenue model thinking  
* Pricing logic  
* Basic financial planning

4️⃣ **Operations**

* Managing progress  
* Coordinating work  
* Maintaining momentum  
* Keeping the company organized

In very early startups, **one person may fill multiple roles.**

But all roles must exist.

---

## **Reflection Questions**

1. Which area are you strongest in?  
2. Which area are you weakest in?  
3. Which area would most limit your progress?

## **Exercise**

Rate your team’s current capability from 1-5 in each area.

* Product / R\&D  
* Market & Sales  
* Financial Thinking  
* Operations / Execution
