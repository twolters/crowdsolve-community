# Lesson 1 \- Describe the solution in your own words

## **Framing:**

* You probably already have a solution in mind. That’s fine.  
* Your job is not to defend it.  
* Your job is to express it as a **hypothesis that can be proven wrong.**

## **Prompt:**

In plain language, describe the solution you want to build.

Rules:

* No constraints.  
* No formatting.  
* Just explain it like you would to a friend.

## **Final Deliverable (founder submits):**

Solution statement in your own words

## **Get Marvin Feedback**

1. Create a new comment on your startup cohort post add the text, “@marvin mm\_s1\_m2\_l1”  
2. Follow that by typing in your Problem Statemet from M1 and then your Solution Statement  
3. Marvin will send feedback as a reply to your comment  
4. Create a new section in your post called, “Refined Solution Hypothesis” with H2 format and underneath that add your solution hypothesis taken from your solution and Marvin’s feedback.
