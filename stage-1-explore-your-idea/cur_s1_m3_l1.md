## **Lesson 1 \- Visionary or Operator**

## **Why This Module Matters**

Many startups fail because:

* Founders try to do everything  
* Founders misjudge their own abilities  
* Founders avoid confronting weaknesses

This module helps founders **see the team they need before they build the team.**

## **Goal**

Understand your natural role as a founder and how you will lead the company.

Based on **EOS / Traction** principles, startups need two very different leadership modes:

**Visionary**

* Generates ideas  
* Sees opportunities  
* Sets direction  
* Inspires others  
* Thinks about the future

**Operator (Integrator)**

* Turns ideas into plans  
* Manages execution  
* Builds systems  
* Keeps teams aligned  
* Ensures progress happens

Most founders are **strongly one or the other.**

Startups struggle when the founder tries to play a role they are not suited for.

---

## **Reflection Questions**

1. What parts of building a company energize you?  
2. What parts drain your energy?  
3. In past work, were you known for ideas or execution?  
4. Do you prefer creating direction or managing progress?
