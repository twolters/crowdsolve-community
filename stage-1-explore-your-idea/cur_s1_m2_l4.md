# Lesson 4 \- The hypothesis formula

*Now formalize it into Lean Startup language.*

### **Use this template:**

We believe that  
\[specific customer\]  
who struggles with \[validated problem\]  
will achieve \[desired outcome\]  
if we provide \[core solution mechanism\].

## **Example Output:**

*We believe that first-time Shopify store owners doing $5k–$20k/month who manually manage fulfillment will reduce late shipments if we provide automated fulfillment workflow tracking.*

## **This should be now:**

* Testable  
* Specific  
* Grounded
