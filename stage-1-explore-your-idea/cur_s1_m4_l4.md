# Lesson 4 \- Turning Your Story Into an Early Pitch Deck

### **Goal**

Transform your startup story into a simple pitch deck that helps you communicate your idea clearly to others.

At this stage of your startup, the purpose of a pitch deck is **not fundraising**.

Instead, it is a tool to help you:

* Explain your idea clearly  
* Recruit potential cofounders  
* Get advice from mentors or investors  
* Introduce your startup to potential customers  
* Generate interest in helping you move forward

The deck helps you turn the narrative you developed in the previous lessons into a **visual story that others can quickly understand.**

You have already written the three most important components of the story:

* **Act 1 — The Problem**  
* **Act 2 — The Solution**  
* **Act 3 — The Vision**

In this lesson, the Mechanical Mentor will help convert these elements into a **clear early pitch deck structure.**

The goal is not perfection.  
The goal is **clarity and momentum.**

Your deck will evolve many times as you learn more about the market.
