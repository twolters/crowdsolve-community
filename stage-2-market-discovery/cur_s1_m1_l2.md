# Lesson 2 \- Define customer segments

## **Goal**

Clearly define **who experiences the problem**.

A common mistake is describing a broad market instead of a **specific early adopter**.

---

## **Exercise**

Answer the following:

1️⃣ Who experiences the problem?  
2️⃣ Who actually uses the solution?  
3️⃣ Who decides to pay for it?

---

## **Template**

Primary Customer Segment  
(Who has the problem)

End User  
(Who experiences the pain)

Economic Buyer  
(Who pays for the solution)

---

## **Guidance**

Focus on:

• roles, not demographics  
• specific environments or contexts  
• reachable people (you can talk to them soon)

Avoid:

• “small businesses”  
• “everyone”  
• “any company”

---

## **Example**

Primary Customer  
Operations managers at mid-sized logistics companies

End User  
Warehouse supervisors

Economic Buyer  
Head of Operations

---

## **Founder Homework**

A clear description of:

• target customer  
• end user  
• economic buyer
