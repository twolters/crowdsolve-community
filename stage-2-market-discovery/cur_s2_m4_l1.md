## **Module Overview**

Up to this point, you’ve:

• defined your problem and customer  
• spoken with real customers  
• identified patterns and insights  
• mapped the competitive landscape

Now it’s time to answer a critical question:

**Do people care enough to take action?**

---

## **Module Goal**

Test whether your idea generates **real interest**, not just conversation.

This is your first step toward:

👉 **real traction signals**

---

## **What This Module Is (and Isn’t)**

This is NOT about:

• building a full product  
• perfect branding  
• launching publicly

This IS about:

• testing interest quickly  
• learning what messaging works  
• seeing if people take action

---

## **Key Principle**

Interest is not what people say.  
👉 Interest is what people DO.

---

## **Lesson 1 — Landing Page Test**

### **Goal**

Create a simple page that communicates your idea clearly and invites action.

---

### **What to Include**

Your landing page should answer:

---

**1\. The Problem**

What is happening in the customer’s world?

Use real language from your interviews.

---

**2\. The Value Proposition**

What outcome are you helping them achieve?

Focus on results, not features.

---

**3\. The Solution Concept**

How might this work (at a high level)?

Keep it simple.

---

**4\. Call to Action (CTA)**

Give people a clear next step:

• Join a waitlist  
• Request early access  
• Sign up for updates

---

## **Important Guidelines**

• Keep it simple — this is a test, not a launch  
• One page is enough  
• Clarity \> design  
• Use real customer language
