# Lesson 3 \- Define the unique value proposition

## **Goal**

Create a **clear, simple statement** that explains why your solution matters.

This is not a feature list — it is a **promise of value**.

---

## **Exercise**

Complete the sentence:

We help **\[customer\]**  
solve **\[problem\]**  
so they can **\[desired outcome\]**

---

## **Guidance**

Your value proposition should be:

• simple and easy to understand  
• focused on outcomes  
• specific to your customer

Avoid:

• buzzwords  
• vague benefits like “improve efficiency”

---

## **Example**

We help small e-commerce brands reduce shipping errors so they can deliver orders faster and improve customer satisfaction.

---
