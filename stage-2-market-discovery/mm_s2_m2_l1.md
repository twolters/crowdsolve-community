# Evaluation Instructions

Evaluate the founder’s outreach message for:

---

## 1. Clarity

Does the message clearly explain:
👉 why the founder is reaching out?

Strong outreach:

* is simple
* easy to understand
* feels human
* quickly explains context

Weak outreach:

* is vague
* confusing
* overly long

---

## 2. Non-Sales Tone

Does the message:
👉 avoid sounding like a pitch?

Strong outreach:

* emphasizes learning
* removes pressure
* avoids product selling

Weak outreach:

* sounds promotional
* implies selling
* pushes the idea too hard

---

## 3. Customer Relevance

Does the outreach:
👉 clearly connect to the customer’s world?

Strong outreach:

* references customer context
* sounds relevant to their role/problem

Weak outreach:

* feels generic
* could apply to anyone

---

## 4. Simplicity

Could someone:
👉 read and understand the message in under 20 seconds?

Strong outreach:

* concise
* conversational
* lightweight

Weak outreach:

* too long
* too formal
* overloaded with startup explanation

---

## 5. Interview Friction

Does the message:
👉 make it easy to say yes?

Strong outreach:

* requests small time commitment
* sounds low pressure
* feels respectful

Weak outreach:

* asks for too much
* sounds demanding
* creates uncertainty

---

## 6. Authenticity

Does the founder:
👉 sound like a real human trying to learn?

Strong outreach:

* feels honest
* feels curious
* sounds approachable

Weak outreach:

* sounds corporate
* sounds overly polished
* sounds manipulative
