# Lesson 3 \- Estimate Market Size

## **Goal**

Understand whether this opportunity is:

👉 small and niche  
👉 or large and expandable

---

## **Important Reminder**

At this stage:

👉 You are estimating — not proving

Your goal is to understand:

“Is this worth pursuing?”

---

## **The Three Levels of Market Size**

---

### **1\. TAM — Total Addressable Market**

The total number of people or companies who **could experience this problem**.

---

### **2\. SAM — Serviceable Available Market**

The subset of TAM that:

• matches your customer segment that you could realistically reach

---

### **3\. SOM — Serviceable Obtainable Market**

The portion of SAM you could realistically capture early on.

👉 This is your **starting market**

---

## **Exercise**

---

### **Step 1 — Define Your Customer Count**

Estimate:

• how many people or companies match your customer segment

Use:

• industry reports  
• LinkedIn searches  
• public data  
• reasonable assumptions

Write down the broadest definition of potential customers. This is your TAM definition.

Write down the subset of these that match your customer segment in your previous homework. Rewrite that segment definition. This is your SAM definition.

Now write down the easiest subset of your SAM definition that you can get into. This might be geographically, demographically, or psychographically split out. This is where you try to get initial traction. This is your SOM definition.

---

### **Step 2 — Estimate Value per Customer**

Ask:

• how much would a customer pay per year?  
• or how much value is created?

---

### **Step 3 — Calculate Market Sizes**

---

TAM

How many customers are in this very broad sgementation. Total customers × value per customer

---

SAM

How many customers are in this subset of the TAM you can realistically serve?

---

SOM

What is the small % of SAM you could capture early?

---

## **Shopify Example (Simple)**

### **TAM**

All Shopify stores selling physical products worldwide.

* Customer Count: \~2.4M  
* Annual Market Size: \~$1.44B

---

### **SAM**

First-time Shopify stores doing $5k–$20k/month and self-managing fulfillment.

* Customer Count: \~384K  
* Annual Market Size: \~$230M

---

### **SOM**

North American Shopify stores in that segment actively looking for operational tools.

* Customer Count: \~5,760  
* Annual Market Size: \~$3.46M
