# 🧭 CrowdSolve — Founder Operations Track (Runs Parallel to Stage 2)

## 🎯 Purpose

While you’re learning from the market, you’re also quietly preparing:

👉 the *foundation* of a real company

This is NOT about overbuilding.

It’s about:

• making smart early decisions
• avoiding costly mistakes
• being ready when momentum hits

---

# 🧠 Guiding Principle

> Don’t formalize too early…
> But don’t be unprepared when things start working.

# 🗓️ 12-Week Founder Ops To-Do (Parallel Track)

---

# 🟢 Weeks 1–2 — Naming + Intent

## Focus: Identity + Direction

### To-Do

• Brainstorm company name ideas
• Check:

* domain availability
* basic brand conflicts

• Decide:

* is this a project?
* or something you *intend* to turn into a company?

---

### Output

✅ Working company name
✅ Directional intent (hobby vs company)

---

---

# 🟢 Weeks 3–4 — Legal Basics (Light Touch)

## Focus: Understand before acting

### To-Do

Research:

• LLC vs C-Corp (don’t incorporate yet unless needed)
• When incorporation actually matters:

* taking money
* signing contracts
* liability concerns

• Start thinking about:

* cofounders (if any)
* ownership expectations

---

### Output

✅ Basic legal understanding
✅ No unnecessary early complexity

---

---

# 🟡 Weeks 5–6 — Financial Awareness

## Focus: Know your burn before you spend

### To-Do

• List expected early expenses:

* tools
* software
* contractors
* marketing tests

• Create simple model:

👉 Monthly burn estimate

---

### Set Up

• Separate tracking method:

* spreadsheet OR
* simple tool (like Wave)

Track:

• personal money going into startup
👉 treat as **founder loan**

---

### Output

✅ Clear expense awareness
✅ Basic financial discipline

---

---

# 🔵 Weeks 7–8 — Incorporation Decision

## Focus: Decide if/when to formalize

### Trigger Question

👉 “Am I seeing real traction or commitment?”

---

### If YES:

• Choose entity (likely LLC early)
• Incorporate
• Open business bank account

---

### If NO:

• Stay unincorporated
• keep tracking expenses

---

### Also Begin

• Draft simple founder agreement (if multiple founders)

---

### Output

✅ Entity decision (not automatic)
✅ Clean structure if needed

---

---

# 🟣 Weeks 9–10 — Financial System + KPIs

## Focus: Operate like a real business

### To-Do

Define early KPIs:

• # of interviews
• conversion rates
• waitlist growth
• engagement signals

---

### Set Weekly Tracking

Example:

• Outreach → Interviews
• Post → Clicks
• Clicks → Signups

---

### Build Simple Dashboard

👉 Google Sheet is enough

---

### Output

✅ KPI dashboard
✅ Weekly tracking discipline

---
