# Lesson 2 \- Identify Competitors

## **Goal of Lesson 2**

Understand who is already solving this problem — directly or indirectly.

Every meaningful problem already has solutions.

Your job is to map the landscape. You’ve already mapped out what solutions your prospective customers are using in the previous lesson, now let’s refine that to naming your actual competitors.

---

## **Types of Competitors**

You will identify three types:

---

### **1\. Direct Competitors**

Companies that are trying to solve the **same problem in a similar way**.

---

### **2\. Substitute Solutions**

Ways customers solve the problem today, even if not ideal.

Examples:

• spreadsheets  
• internal tools  
• manual workflows  
• hiring people

---

### **3\. Adjacent Approaches**

Companies solving a **related problem** or serving a similar customer.

---

## **Exercise**

Using:

* your Lean Canvas  
* your interview insights  
* and the additional work you did in the previous lesson

Identify at least **5 relevant competitive companies or solutions**.

---

### **Founder Homework**

Submit a bullet list for each competitor with Company Name or Solution Title as the major bullet and following sub-bullets:

URL (if company), Description, Category, Why relevant, Your Solution Advantage, Competitive Type \[Direct, Substitute or Adjacent\]

Is there anything you think you will need to add to your solution to be competitive? Why will customers switch from these solutions to yours?
