# Evaluation Instructions

Evaluate the founder’s work for:

---

## 1. Problem ↔ Solution Alignment

Does each solution:
👉 directly address the stated problem?

Strong alignment:

* clearly reduces customer pain
* improves workflows
* resolves operational friction

Weak alignment:

* introduces unrelated functionality
* feels disconnected from the problem
* sounds overly broad

---

## 2. Conceptual Clarity

Can you quickly understand:
👉 how the solution helps the customer?

Strong solutions:

* stay conceptual
* focus on outcomes
* remain simple

Weak solutions:

* overdescribe features
* become technical
* feel confusing

---

## 3. Customer Outcome Focus

Does the founder explain:
👉 what changes for the customer?

Strong outcomes:

* reduce time
* reduce cost
* reduce stress
* improve reliability
* improve coordination

Weak outcomes:

* focus only on technology
* lack customer impact

---

## 4. Scope Discipline

Does the founder:
👉 remain focused on a manageable solution?

Strong focus:

* solves a narrow painful problem first

Weak focus:

* attempts to become a giant platform immediately
* solves too many problems simultaneously

---

## 5. Lean Canvas Readiness

Does the founder now have:
👉 enough clarity to begin customer interviews?

Look for:

* coherent customer logic
* believable problem/solution fit
* clear value creation
* validation-ready thinking
