# Lesson 2 \- write interview questions

## **Goal**

Learn how to ask questions that uncover **real behavior**, not opinions.

---

### **The Three Rules of The Mom Test**

1️⃣ Talk about their life, not your idea  
2️⃣ Ask about the past, not the future  
3️⃣ Listen for pain and behavior, not compliments

---

### **Good Questions (Examples)**

• When was the last time this problem happened?  
• What did you do to deal with it?  
• What tools or processes did you use?  
• How long did it take?  
• What was frustrating about that process?

---

### **Bad Questions (Avoid These)**

• Would you use a product like this?  
• Do you think this is a good idea?  
• Would you pay for this?

👉 These lead to polite, misleading answers.
