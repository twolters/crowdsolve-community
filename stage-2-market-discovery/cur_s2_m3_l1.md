## Module Overview and Lesson 1 \- Analyze the solution space

## **Module Overview**

You’ve now:

• defined your problem and customer  
• spoken with real customers  
• identified patterns and insights

Now you need to understand:

👉 **What already exists in the market?**

👉 **Who will be your chief competitors?**

👉 **How big is this opportunity?**

This module helps you step back and answer:

“Where does my idea fit in the real world?”

---

## **What This Module Is (and Isn’t)**

This is NOT about:

• proving your market is huge  
• building a perfect competitive analysis  
• impressing investors

This IS about:

• understanding how the problem is solved today  
• identifying where your idea fits  
• estimating whether this is a meaningful opportunity

---

## **Goal of Lesson 1**

Understand **how the problem is actually solved today**.

This is one of the most important steps in Market Discovery.

---

## **Key Question**

👉 “What do people actually do right now?”

---

## **Exercise**

Using your interview notes, answer:

---

### **1\. What tools or services do customers use?**

Examples:

* software platforms  
* consultants  
* agencies  
* internal system  
* ignore the problem altogether (not a good sign)

---

### **2\. What manual workarounds exist?**

Examples:

• spreadsheets  
• email coordination  
• manual tracking  
• custom processes

---

### **3\. What are the limitations?**

For each solution, identify:

• what doesn’t work  
• where frustration exists  
• where time or money is wasted

---

### **4\. Why haven’t they solved this better already?**

This is critical.

Possible answers:

• too expensive  
• too complex  
• no good alternatives  
• not urgent enough

---
